CS50x Final Project: News Radio

For my final project for CS50, I created a webpage designed to give a streamlined view of the top headlines of the day and my twitter feed. I then connected to an API that lets you chose various voices to read back the webpage to you. This allowed me to create a custom news radio feed that I could play in the background while running or driving.
